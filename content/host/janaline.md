Janaline is a former diplomat and current climate, environment and anti-racism activist. As a longstanding Canberra-based bureaucrat, she believes in the power of policy to shape and improve lives. She is also acutely aware of the importance of having those policies understood by the people affected by them. She started Why Can't They Just? as way of moving beyond slogans and into what policies really are and what they mean for real people.

Glenn has a background in education, public service and community radio. After far too long being annoyed about the confected outrage, gaslighting, punching down and wilful distortion of facts in our national discourse, he jumped at the opportunity to join the team at Policy for People and Why Can't They Just. Glenn hopes to contribute something positive to ordinary people like him understanding complex issues and exercising their vote in an informed way to build and sustain a community and nation that works for all of us.
